When will Apex Console be available?

Apex Console is launching globally in Q2 2025. Pre-orders are available now with exclusive launch bonuses including a limited edition controller, 3 free games, and 6 months of Apex Plus membership.

What's included in the box?

Each Apex Console package includes the main console unit, one wireless controller, HDMI 2.1 cable, power cable, USB-C charging cable, quick start guide, and exclusive digital content codes.

What are the technical specifications?

Apex Console features a 16.2 TFLOPS custom GPU, 8-core Zen 4 CPU @ 4.0GHz, 24GB GDDR6 RAM, 2TB NVMe SSD with 7GB/s read speeds, and supports 8K resolution at 120fps with real-time ray tracing.

Is Apex Console backward compatible?

Yes! Apex Console is fully backward compatible with thousands of titles from previous generations. Classic games run with enhanced performance, improved framerates, and higher resolutions.

How much storage space is available?

The console comes with a 2TB ultra-fast SSD. Storage is expandable via the dedicated expansion slot, supporting officially licensed SSD expansion cards up to 4TB.

Does it support 4K and 8K gaming?

Absolutely. Apex Console natively supports 4K at 120fps and 8K at 60fps, along with variable refresh rate (VRR), HDR10, and Dolby Vision for stunning visual fidelity.

What is Apex Plus membership?

Apex Plus is our premium subscription service offering 2-4 free games monthly, exclusive discounts, cloud gaming, early access to new releases, and online multiplayer. Plans start at $9.99/month.

Can I play online without a subscription?

Free-to-play games and single-player experiences don't require a subscription. Apex Plus membership is only needed for online multiplayer in premium titles and accessing monthly free games.

What controllers are compatible?

Apex Console supports our official wireless controllers, previous generation controllers via adapter, and most third-party USB controllers. The adaptive controller features haptic feedback and adaptive triggers.

Is the console region locked?

No. Apex Console and all games are region-free, allowing you to play any game from any region on your console without restrictions.

How does cloud gaming work?

With Apex Plus Pro or Ultimate membership, stream your entire game library to compatible devices including phones, tablets, and laptops. Your saves sync automatically across all platforms.

What's the return policy for pre-orders?

Pre-orders can be cancelled anytime before shipment for a full refund. After delivery, you have 30 days to return the console in original condition for a full refund.

How loud is the console during operation?

Apex Console features an advanced thermal management system with whisper-quiet operation. Even during demanding 8K gaming sessions, the console remains virtually silent at under 38 decibels.

Does it support VR gaming?

Yes! Apex Console is VR-ready and compatible with major VR headsets. Our hardware delivers smooth, high-fidelity VR experiences with low latency and high framerates.

What warranty is included?

Every Apex Console includes a 1-year limited hardware warranty. Extended warranty plans (2-year and 3-year options) are available at checkout for additional protection and priority support.

Can I use my existing game library?

Digital games from your existing account transfer automatically. Physical disc-based games from previous generations work seamlessly with backward compatibility enhancements.

How do I contact customer support?

We'd love to hear from you! Please use the contact form on our home page, email support@apexconsole.com, or chat with our support team 24/7 through your account dashboard.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and financing options through our partners. Pay later plans with 0% APR are available for qualified buyers on pre-orders.
